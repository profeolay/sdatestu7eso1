Mission 2: The Time Travel Podcast🎙️

Lights, microphone, action! 🎬

Welcome to your second mission. It is time to travel to the past and use your voice! You are going to record a short radio interview.


👥 How to work?

You will work in your cooperative pairs randomly chosen. You have two roles:

Student A (The Journalist): You work for National Geographic. You ask the questions.


Student B (The Explorer): You are the famous explorer from your Infographic. You answer the questions.

📝 Step 1: The Script 

Work together to write the interview.

Write 4 questions and 4 answers.
You must use the Past Simple (was/were) and Adjectives (-ed / -ing).
 

Example:

Journalist: Were you frightened in the expedition?
Explorer: Yes, I was terrified! The mountain was very dangerous.


🗣️ Step 2: Practice! 

Practice your dialogue. Remember to use a loud and clear voice. You are actors!

🎧 Step 3: Recording and Editing 

  • Open the webpage Vocaroo on the school computer or Audacity.
  • Record your interview.
  • Extra challenge: Add a background sound effect (wind, ocean, jungle...) from Freesound.org to make it realistic. 

📤 Step 4: Submit your mission (Entrega)

When your podcast is ready, export the file as an .mp3.

Go to Microsoft Teams.
Upload your audio to: General Channel > Files > Podcasts 1º ESO.

Remember to name it including both your and your classmate's Name and Surname. As in the following example: MARÍA_ANTEQUERA_PLATAS_and_ULISES_RODRÍGUEZ_PÉREZ.mp3

🏆 How will this mission be evaluated? The teacher will use a Rubric to evaluate your podcast. We will check:

Grammar: Correct use of was/were and vocabulary.
Pronunciation: Clear voice and good intonation.
Creativity: Acting and sound effects.

Good luck, time travellers! ⏱️🚀
