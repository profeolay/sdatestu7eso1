Mission 1 - Interactive Infographic

Mission 1 - Interactive Infographic

Let's do it! You are going to design a visual biography of your explorer.

Where?

- Use Canva or Genially.
What to include?

A free photo (Creative Commons) of your explorer.
At least three interactive buttons with interesting facts.
Short texts using was/were and adjectives (-ed/-ing).

How to send it? Finish your design, copy the public link, and paste it in Microsoft Teams (General Channel > Assignments > Mission 1).
