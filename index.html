Introduction

Hello, explorers! In the next two weeks, we are going to travel in time. We will learn about great explorers and their discoveries. But we are not going to read a boring book... You are going to be the creators!

What is our mission? At the end of this journey, you will work in pairs to create two big projects:

  • An Interactive Infographic about a famous explorer (like Amelia Earhart, Ibn Battuta, or Edmund Hillary).
  • A "Time Travel" Podcast: A radio interview.

Are you ready for the adventure? Let's go!
